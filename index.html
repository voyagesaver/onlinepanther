What Is Online Panther?

BLACK PANTHER ONLINE is a speaking service providing webinars analyzing political lessons from the experience of the [original, Oakland-based] Black Panther Party, including online Movie Talks as well as full online Speaking Presentations to students, educational institutions, and community organizations by former Black Panther Charles “Cappy” Pinderhughes, Ph.D.

Online Speaking Presentations

Have Dr. Pinderhughes, former Oakland Black Panther Party member (liteutenant of information), make a full-length presentation on your choice of topic about the Black Panther Party.

Movie Talks

 After viewing Documentaries or Movies about the Black Panther Party, Provide your students with the opportunity to hear and question a former Black Panther for more inclusive insights

About Dr. Pinderhughes

Charles "Cappy" Pinderhughes, Ph.D. a veteran Black community and labor activist for over thirty years, was Lieutenant of Information for the New Haven Chapter of the Black Panther Party in 1969 and 1970 during the pretrial phase of the campaign to free Bobby Seale and the New Haven Panthers. Dr. Pinderhughes developed The People's News Service, a Panther periodical and organizing tool consisting of mostly local news. That organ was widely copied by other Panther chapters across the country. A native of Boston MA, Dr. Pinderhughes has also lived, worked and organized in Beloit WI, Baltimore MD and Atlanta GA. He now resides in Newark NJ where he teaches at Essex County College. He earned a Masters in Political Science from Goddard College, and his Ph.D. in Sociology from Boston College in 2009. His fields of specialization focus on Racial & Ethnic Relations, Historical Sociology, Social Movements, Black Power Studies and Anti-Colonial Marxism.
